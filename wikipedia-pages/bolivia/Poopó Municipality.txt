'''Poopó Municipality''' is the first [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Poopó Province]] in the [[Oruro Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its capital is Poopó.<ref name=ine>{{cite web|url=http://www.ine.gob.bo/publicaciones/visorPdf.aspx?Codigo=040601&tipo=1|title=Poopó|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|access-date=October 15, 2015|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304072010/http://www.ine.gob.bo/publicaciones/visorPdf.aspx?Codigo=040601&tipo=1|archive-date=March 4, 2016}}</ref>

== Geography ==
The municipality lies east of the [[Desaguadero River (Bolivia)|Desaguadero]] River where it connects [[Uru Uru Lake]] and [[Poopó Lake]].

== Subdivision ==
The municipality is divided into three [[Cantons of Bolivia|cantons]].
*Coripata Canton
*Poopó Canton
*Venta y Media Canton

== Languages ==
The languages spoken in the Poopó Municipality are mainly [[Spanish language|Spanish]], [[Quechua languages|Quechua]] and [[Aymara language|Aymara]] .
