'''Tacopaya Municipality''' is the second [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Arque Province]] in the [[Cochabamba Department]] in [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[Tacopaya]]. At the time of census 2001 the municipality had 11,658 inhabitants.<ref name="[http://www.ine.gov.bo]"> [http://www.ine.gov.bo Instituto Nacional de Estadística Bolivia (INE) 2001] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091027220541/http://www.ine.gov.bo/ |date=2009-10-27 }} (Spanish)</ref>

== Languages ==
The languages spoken in the Tacopaya Municipality are mainly [[Quechua languages|Quechua]] and [[Spanish language|Spanish]].<ref>[http://obd.descentralizacion.gov.bo/municipal/fichas/ obd.descentralizacion.gov.bo / Observatorio Bolivia Democrático] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090218081859/http://obd.descentralizacion.gov.bo/municipal/fichas/ |date=2009-02-18 }} (Spanish)</ref>
