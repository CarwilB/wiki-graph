'''Guanay Municipality''' ([[Aymara language|Aymara]]: ''Wanay'') is the second [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Larecaja Province]] in the  [[La Paz Department (Bolivia)|La Paz Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[Guanay]].

== Geography ==
The [[Cordillera Real (Bolivia)|Cordillera Real]] traverses the Guanay Municipality. Some of the highest peaks of the range,  [[Chachakumani]] and [[Ch'iyar Juqhu]], lie within the borders of the municipality.
