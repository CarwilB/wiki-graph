'''Santa Rosa''' or '''Santa Rosa de(l) Yacuma''' is a [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipality]] of the [[José Ballivián Province]] in the [[Beni Department]] of [[Bolivia]]. The seat of the municipality is the town of [[Santa Rosa de Yacuma]].
