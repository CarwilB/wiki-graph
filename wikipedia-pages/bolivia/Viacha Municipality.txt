'''Viacha Municipality''' is the first [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Ingavi Province]] in the  [[La Paz Department (Bolivia)|La Paz Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[Viacha]].

== Division ==
The municipality is subdivided into seven [[Cantons of Bolivia|cantons]]:
*Chacoma Irpa Grande - 1,247 inhabitants (2001) <ref>[http://www.ine.gob.bo National Institute of Statistic of Bolivia]  {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110924073930/http://www.ine.gob.bo/ |date=September 24, 2011 }} </ref>
*General José Ballivian - 452 inhabitants  
*Ichuraya Grande - 332 inhabitants
*Irpuma Irpa Grande - 1,078 inhabitants
*Viacha - 46,596 inhabitants
*Villa Remedios - 1,449 inhabitants
*Villa Santiago de Chacoma - 438 inhabitants

== The people ==
The people are predominantly [[Indigenous peoples of the Americas|indigenous]] citizens of [[Aymara people|Aymara]] descent.<ref>obd.descentralizacion.gov.bo/municipal/fichas/ (inactive)</ref>
