'''Ancoraimes''' ([[Aymara language|Aymara]]: ''Janq'u Laymi'') is the second [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Omasuyos Province]] in the  [[La Paz Department (Bolivia)|La Paz Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[Ancoraimes]] (Janq'u Laymi).
