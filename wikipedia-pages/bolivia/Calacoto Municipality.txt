'''Calacoto''' or '''Qala Qutu''' ([[Aymara language|Aymara]] ''qala'' stone, ''qutu'' heap, pile,<ref name=bertonio/> "stone pile") is the third [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Pacajes Province]] in the  [[La Paz Department (Bolivia)|La Paz Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is Calacoto (Qala Qutu).
