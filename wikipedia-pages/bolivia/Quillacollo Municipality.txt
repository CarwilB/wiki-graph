'''Quillacollo Municipality''' is the first [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Quillacollo Province]] in the [[Cochabamba Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its capital is [[Quillacollo]]. At the time of census 2001 the municipality had 104,206 inhabitants.<ref>{{in lang|es}} [http://www.ine.gov.bo Instituto Nacional de Estadística]  {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091113141644/http://www.ine.gov.bo/ |date=November 13, 2009 }}</ref>
