'''Rurrenabaque''' is a [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipality]] in the [[José Ballivián Province]] in the [[Beni Department]] of [[Bolivia]]. The seat of the municipality is the town of [[Rurrenabaque]]. At the 2001 census the municipality had 14,000 inhabitants, of which 8,000 lived in the town of Rurrenabaque.<ref name="INE" />
