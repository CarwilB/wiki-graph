'''Tiraque Municipality''' is the first [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Tiraque Province]] in the [[Cochabamba Department]], [[Bolivia]]. At the time of census 2001 - still including Shinahota Canton (now [[Shinahota Municipality]]) - it had a population of 35,017. Its seat is [[Tiraque]].

== Cantons ==
The municipality is divided into two cantons. They are (their seats in parentheses):
* [[Palca Canton (Cochabamba)|Palca Canton]] - (Palca)
* [[Tiraque Canton]] - ([[Tiraque]])

In 2009 Shinahota Canton (also named Central Busch Canton) got the status of a municipality. <ref> [http://www.planguarani.com/noticias/pagina.php?sip=246 www.planguarani.com] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110715092055/http://www.planguarani.com/noticias/pagina.php?sip=246 |date=2011-07-15 }} (Spanish)</ref> Now Shinahota Municipality with its seat [[Shinahota]] is the second municipal section of the Tiraque Province.
