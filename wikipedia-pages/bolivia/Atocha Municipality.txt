'''Atocha Municipality''' is the second [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Sud Chichas Province]] in the [[Potosí Department]] in [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[Atocha, Bolivia|Atocha]]. It is located within the [[Andes]] mountain range.

== Subdivision ==
Atocha Municipality was created on September 21, 1963 by Law No. 245<ref>[http://www.legislacionmunicipal.fam.bo/Archivo/Docs/Leyes/Ley_245.pdf Law No. 245, ''Ley de 21 de septiembre de 1963''] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120326031749/http://www.legislacionmunicipal.fam.bo/Archivo/Docs/Leyes/Ley_245.pdf |date=March 26, 2012 }}</ref>  consisting of the following seven cantons: Chocaya, Portugalete, San Vicente, Santa Bárbara, Tacmari, Guadalupe and Chorolque Viejo.

The National Institute of Statistics of Bolivia INE lists eight divisions of the municipality of Atocha:<ref>[http://www.ine.gob.bo:8082/censo/make_table.jsp INE, Bolivia, Division of Atocha Municipality: Population Data]{{dead link|date=October 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> [[Atocha Canton|Atocha]], [[Chorolque Canton|Chorolque]], [[Chorolque Viejo Canton|Chorolque Viejo]], [[Portugalete Canton|Portugalete]], [[Chocaya Canton|Chocaya]], [[Guadalupe Canton, Sud Chichas|Guadalupe]], [[San Vicente Canton, Bolivia|San Vicente]], [[Santa Bárbara (Potosí)|Santa Bárbara]].

== The people ==
The people are predominantly [[Indigenous peoples of the Americas|indigenous]] citizens of [[Quechua people|Quechua]] descent.<ref>obd.descentralizacion.gov.bo/municipal/fichas/ obd.descentralizacion.gov.bo</ref>
