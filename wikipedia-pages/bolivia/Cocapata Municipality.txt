'''Cocapata Municipality''' ({{langx|qu|Kukapata}}) is the third [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Ayopaya Province]] in the [[Cochabamba Department]] in central [[Bolivia]].<ref>[http://www.descentralizacion.org.bo/index.php?pg=/noticias/Nuevosmunicipioscreadosentre2005y2009/ www.descentralizacion.org.bo]{{dead link|date=August 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} (Spanish)</ref><ref>[http://www.bolivia.com/noticIAS/AutoNoticias/DetalleNoticia42293.asp www.bolivia.com] (Spanish)</ref> Its seat [[Cocapata]] had 411 inhabitants at the time of census 2001.<ref>[http://www.ine.gov.bo/ Instituto Nacional de Estadistica de Bolivia] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091027220541/http://www.ine.gov.bo/ |date=October 27, 2009 }} (Spanish)</ref>

Cocapata has many tourist attractions, as rock art (painting rocks) andean bear o (kucumari) the wild vicuñas in the region altamachi, many natural lakes which it has fish truchas and a variety of cultural heritage.
