'''Colquiri Municipality''' is the fourth [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Inquisivi Province]] in the  [[La Paz Department (Bolivia)|La Paz Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[Colquiri]].
