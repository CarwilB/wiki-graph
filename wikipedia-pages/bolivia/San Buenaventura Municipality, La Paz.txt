'''San Buenaventura Municipality''' is the second [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Abel Iturralde Province]] located in the [[La Paz Department, Bolivia|La Paz Department]] in [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[San Buenaventura, La Paz|San Buenaventura]].

== Languages ==
The languages spoken in the San Buenaventura Municipality are primarily [[Spanish language|Spanish]] and [[Quechua languages|Quechua]].
