'''San Benito Municipality''' or '''Villa José Quintín Mendoza Municipality''' is the third [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Punata Province]] in the [[Cochabamba Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[San Benito, Cochabamba|San Benito]]. At the time of census 2001 the municipality had 12,720 inhabitants.<ref>[http://www.ine.gov.bo Instituto Nacional de Estadística] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091027220541/http://www.ine.gov.bo/ |date=2009-10-27 }} (Spanish)</ref>

== Cantons ==
[[File:Mapa politico punata.JPG|thumb|left|160px|Political map of Punata Province]]
The municipality is divided into three cantons. They are (their seats in parentheses):
* [[Huaricaya Canton]] or San Lorenzo Canton - (San Lorenzo)
* [[San Benito Canton]] or Villa José Quintín Mendoza Canton - ([[San Benito, Cochabamba|San Benito]])
* [[Sunchu Pampa Canton]] - (Sunchu Pampa)
