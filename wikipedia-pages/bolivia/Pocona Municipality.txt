'''Pocona Municipality''' is the third [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Carrasco Province (Bolivia)|Carrasco Province]] in the [[Cochabamba Department]] in [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[Pocona]]. At the time of census 2001 the municipality had 13,488 inhabitants.<ref>[http://www.ine.gov.bo/comunitaria/comunitariaVer.aspx?Depto=03&Prov=12&Seccion=00 Instituto Nacional de Estadística Bolivia (INE) 2001]</ref>

== Economy ==
The principal economic activities are the cultivation of stone fruit and potatoes and tourism.

== Places of interest ==
The monumental [[Inca]] site of [[Inkallajta|Inkallaqta]] is situated in the Pocona Municipality.
