'''Potosí Municipality''' is the capital [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipality]] of the [[Tomás Frías Province]] in the [[Potosí Department]] in [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[Potosí]] which is the capital of the department as well.

== Geography ==
The [[Potosí mountain range]] traverses the municipality. == Subdivision ==
The municipality consists of the following cantons: 
* Chulchucani
* Wari Wari
* Potosí
* [[Tarapaya Canton|Tarapaya]]

== Demographics ==
The people are predominantly [[Indigenous peoples of the Americas|indigenous]] citizens of [[Quechua people|Quechua]] descent.<ref>obd.descentralizacion.gov.bo/municipal/fichas/ (inactive)</ref>
