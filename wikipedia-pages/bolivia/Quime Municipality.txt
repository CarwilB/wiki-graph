'''Quime Municipality''' is the second [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Inquisivi Province]] in the [[La Paz Department, Bolivia|La Paz Department]] in [[Bolivia]], located south-east of the city of La Paz. Its seat is [[Quime]]. The coordinates are  16° 59' 0" South, 67° 13' 0".

== Geography ==
There are three rivers nearby, the Quime River, Chanvillalla River, and Chichipata River.{{Citation needed|date=July 2024}} The [[Kimsa Cruz mountain range|Kimsa Cruz]] mountain range traverses the municipality.<ref>Evelio Echevarría, Cordillera Quimsa Cruz, Bolivia, see sketch map p. 153</ref><ref>Bolivian IGM map 1:50,000 Estancia Choquetanga Chico 6143-IV</ref><ref>IGM map 1:50,000 6142-IV 'Yaco'</ref>

== Division ==
The municipality consists of the following [[Cantons of Bolivia|cantons]]:<ref>[http://www.ine.gob.bo National Institute of Statistic in Bolivia]  {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110924073930/http://www.ine.gob.bo/ |date=September 24, 2011 }}</ref>
* Choquetanga Canton - 2,537 inhabitants ''(2001)'' 
* Figueroa Canton - 149 inhabitants 
* Waña Quta Canton - 69 inhabitants
* Quime Canton - 4,583 inhabitants
