'''San Borja''' is the second [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[José Ballivián Province]] in the [[Beni Department]] of [[Bolivia]]. The seat of the municipality is the town of [[San Borja, Bolivia|San Borja]].

== Languages ==
The languages spoken in the San Borja Municipality are mainly [[Spanish language|Spanish]], [[Chimane]], [[Aymara language|Aymara]] and [[Quechua languages|Quechua]].
