[[File:Isla Chelleca - Lago Titicaca - Bolivia.jpg|thumb|right|200px|Chelleca island, Lake Titicaca]]
'''Copacabana Municipality''' is the first [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Manco Kapac Province]] in the [[La Paz Department (Bolivia)|La Paz Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its capital is [[Copacabana, Bolivia|Copacabana]].

[[Isla del Sol]] (''Island of the Sun'') and [[Chelleca]] island are situated within the municipality.

== Subdivision ==
The municipality was formerly, before the abolition of Bolivian cantons in 2009, divided into three cantons.
