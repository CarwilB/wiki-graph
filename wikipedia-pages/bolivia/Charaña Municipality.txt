'''Charaña Municipality''' is the fifth [[Municipalities of Bolivia|municipal section]] of the [[Pacajes Province]] in the  [[La Paz Department (Bolivia)|La Paz Department]], [[Bolivia]]. Its seat is [[Charaña]].

==Climate==
The climate in Charaña is characterized by a sub-freezing mean annual temperature, with large annual temperature ranges, and moderately low precipitation. The [[Köppen Climate Classification|Köppen Climate System]] classifies this as a [[Tundra climate]], abbreviated as ''ET''.<ref>[http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=3258&cityname=Charana%2C+La+Paz%2C+Bolivia&units= Climate Summary for Charaña Municipality]</ref>
