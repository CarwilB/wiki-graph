'''Puerto Fernández Alonso''' is a municipality in the province [[Obispo Santistevan Province|Obispo Santistevan]] in the department [[Santa Cruz Department (Bolivia)|Santa Cruz]] in [[Bolivia]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Puerto Fernández Alonso en la region de Santa Cruz - Municipio y alcaldía de Bolivia |url=https://www.municipio.com.bo/municipio-puerto-fernandez-alonso.html |access-date=2023-01-15 |website=www.municipio.com.bo}}</ref> It is also known as Fernández Alonso.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |title=Fernández Alonso |url=https://amdecruz.com/municipios/fernandezalonso.php |access-date=2023-01-23 |website=amdecruz.com}}</ref>

== History ==
The municipality was created by Law 2320 on 28 January 2002.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Bolivia: Ley Nº 2320, 28 de enero de 2002 |url=https://www.lexivox.org/norms/BO-L-2320.html |access-date=2023-01-23 |website=www.lexivox.org}}</ref> It comprised two [[Cantons of Bolivia|cantons]]: 

* [[Fernández Alonso]]
* [[Chane Independencia|Chané Independencia]] 
Before 2002, these localities were part of the municipality of Mineros.<ref name=":0" />
