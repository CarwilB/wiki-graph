### ICE Detention Statistics Data Dictionary

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **ADP** | Average daily population |
| **ALIP** | Average length in program |
| **ALOS** | Average length of stay |
| **AOR** | Area of Responsibility |
| **ATD** | Alternatives to Detention |
| **Bonded Out-FO** | An alien is bonded out due to decision by the Field Office |
| **Bonded Out-IJ** | An alien is bonded out due to decision by the Immigration Judge |
| **CBP** | Customs and Border Protection |
| **Classification Level** | Upon admission and periodically thereafter, detainees are categorized into a security level based on a variety of public safety factors (prior convictions, threat risk, disciplinary record, etc.) and housed accordingly. |
| **Classification Level A** | Low security risk |
| **Classification Level B** | Medium-low security risk |
| **Classification Level C** | Medium-high security risk |
| **Classification Level D** | High security risk |
| **Credible Fear** | A finding by USCIS or an Immigration Judge that there is a significant possibility that alien could establish eligibility for asylum under INA § 208, withholding of removal under INA § 241(B)(3), or protection from removal under the convention against torture. |
| **FCMP** | Family Case Management Program |
| **Facility Operator** | The entity responsible for the day-to-day operation of the facility. |
| **Facility Type - BOP** | Bureau of Prisons |
| **Facility Type - CDF** | Contract Detention Facility |
| **Facility Type - Dedicated** | A facility that houses only ICE detainees |
| **Facility Type - DIGSA** | Dedicated Intergovernmental Service Agreement |
| **Facility Type - IGSA** | Intergovernmental Service Agreement |
| **Facility Type - ORR** | Office of Refugee Resettlement |
| **Facility Type - SPC** | Service Processing Center |
| **Facility Type - USMS IGA** | US Marshals Service Intergovernmental Agreement |
| **Final Book-out** | A book-out that results in a terminal release from ICE custody (e.g., removal, death, voluntary return, release). |
| **Initial Book-in** | The first time an individual is booked into ICE custody for a specific arrest. |
| **Inspection Final Rating - Acceptable** | The facility meets the requirements of the applicable detention standards. |
| **Inspection Final Rating - Deficient** | The facility does not meet the requirements of one or more applicable detention standards. |
| **Inspection Final Rating - At-Risk** | The facility has ceased operations or is operating under a suspended/restricted license. |
| **Total Detained** | The total number of aliens currently in ICE custody. |
| **Total Book-ins** | The total number of times aliens were booked into ICE custody during the reporting period. |
| **Total Book-outs** | The total number of times aliens were released/removed from ICE custody during the reporting period. |
| **Threat Level 1** | Detainees with a primary conviction of aggravated felony or two or more felonies. |
| **Threat Level 2** | Detainees with a primary conviction of a felony or three or more misdemeanors. |
| **Threat Level 3** | Detainees with a primary conviction of a misdemeanor (less than 3). |
| **No ICE Threat Level** | Detainees with no prior known criminal conviction. |
